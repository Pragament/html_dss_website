ABOUT DELHI SECONDARY SCHOOL

Welcome to The Delhi Secondary school

Delhi Secondary School is a public high school in Sangareddy, Delhi Secondary School High is in the heart of the community, serving students throughout.
Delhi Secondary School seeks to be a safe haven for students, holding fast to the mission to prepare all students for college, leadership and life. Delhi Secondary School High serves families from a cross-section of racial, cultural, and socioeconomic spectrums.

Admissions

School life at Delhi Secondary School is always a lively mix of academics, school events and trips, guest speakers, sports and co-curricular activities, as well as Judaic tradition and religious observance. Delhi Secondary School believes that education should extend beyond the intellectual to include both socially responsible attitudes and thoughtful and appropriate action.

Our philosophy

Every child is born as a curious explorer, joyful prankstar and a problem solver

School Vision

Our Vision is to provide high quality education and childcare in a safe, respectful and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for life-long learning.

School Mission

We believe that a happy child is a successful one. We are committed to providing a positive, safe and stimulating environment for children to learn, where all are valued. We intend that all children should enjoy their learning, achieve their potential and become independent lifelong learners.
